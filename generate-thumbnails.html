Video Thumbnail Generator

This tool will generate thumbnails from your videos and allow you to download them.

DJI_20250511155856_0068_D.mp4



Instructions:

  1. Wait for the video to load
  2. Click "Generate Thumbnail" to capture the current frame
  3. Or use "Capture at Xs" to capture at specific times
  4. Right-click the generated thumbnail and "Save image as..."
  5. Save it in the "thumbnails" folder with the suggested filename
